About

we are KS Art Gallery

Welcome to KS Art Gallery, where creativity meets tradition and innovation intertwines with heritage. Led by the visionary Khadija Shafiq, we are dedicated to curating a captivating journey through the realm of art, specializing in the intricate beauty of calligraphy and the rich tapestry of Islamic art. At KS Art Gallery, we believe in the power of artistic expression to transcend boundaries and connect souls. With a fusion of tradition and modernity, we offer a sanctuary for artists and art enthusiasts alike to immerse themselves in the splendor of creativity.

Meet Our Founder: Khadija Shafiq

Khadija Shafiq, a luminary in the world of art, leads KS Art Gallery with grace and expertise. Her passion for preserving cultural heritage and promoting Islamic art and calligraphy has earned global admiration.
